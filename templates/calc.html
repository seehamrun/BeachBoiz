Calculator

How to use:
1. Find the rate your electrical company charges you for electricity and input it to the text box.
(You can get this by dividing your monthly total by your amount used)

2. Input either the ideal amount of electricity you want to use, or the ideal amount
of money you would like to spend in their respective boxes.

3. Hit calculate to get the amount you didn't put in. This allows you to create
hypothetical scenarios, which can later help you set goals.
