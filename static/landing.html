Think about the environment

Using less energy will not only save you money

By using less energy, you help reduce the amount of CO2 and other harmful substances that are released into the air by power plants.

• In the US, about 81% of all energy comes from the burning of fossil fuels like coal and oil.
• Power plants convert that into the electricity that powers your home.


By reducing your electricity usage, you actually help the environment too!
